Comprehensive Health Assessments

Advanced blood testing, physical evaluation, and personalized medical insights

What is a Comprehensive Health Assessment?

Our comprehensive health assessment is the foundation of personalized medicine. We conduct an extensive evaluation of your health status using advanced blood testing, physical examination, and lifestyle analysis to create a baseline understanding of your current health and identify optimization opportunities.

This assessment goes beyond routine check-ups. We analyze 50+ biomarkers including complete blood count, metabolic panel, hormone levels, inflammatory markers, lipid profile, and thyroid function to give you a complete picture of your health.

What's Included in Your Assessment

A complete evaluation designed to understand your health at the deepest level

Advanced Blood Testing

Comprehensive analysis of 50+ biomarkers including complete blood count, metabolic markers, hormone levels, inflammation markers, lipid profile, and thyroid function.

Physical Examination

Detailed physical evaluation including vital signs, cardiovascular assessment, and clinical examination by our medical specialist.

Body Composition Analysis

Precise measurement of muscle mass, fat distribution, and metabolic markers. DEXA scan available for bone density analysis and advanced body composition assessment.

Lifestyle Assessment

Comprehensive review of nutrition, exercise, sleep quality, stress levels, and lifestyle factors that impact your health and longevity.

Medical Consultation

45-60 minute in-depth consultation with a licensed medical specialist to review findings, discuss health concerns, and answer all your questions.

Personalized Medical Report

Detailed written summary of all findings with personalized recommendations for health optimization, preventive care, and maintenance plans.

Who Should Get a Comprehensive Assessment?

Baseline Screening

Essential first step for establishing your health baseline and identifying any hidden health issues before they become problems.

Longevity Planning

Perfect for those committed to long-term health optimization and wanting to understand factors affecting aging and lifespan.

Unexplained Symptoms

If you've been experiencing fatigue, brain fog, or other symptoms without a clear diagnosis, our comprehensive assessment can help identify root causes.

Performance Optimization

For athletes and high-performers wanting to optimize biomarkers and understand key metrics for peak performance.

Schedule & Prepare

Our Complete Biomarker Panel

50+ biomarkers analyzed to give you complete health insights

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

  • Red blood cells & hemoglobin
  • White blood cells & differential
  • Platelets & hematocrit

Metabolic Panel

  • Glucose & fasting blood sugar
  • Kidney function (creatinine, BUN)
  • Liver function tests
  • Electrolytes & minerals

Lipid Panel

  • Total cholesterol
  • LDL, HDL, triglycerides
  • Apolipoprotein analysis

Hormone Assessment

  • Thyroid hormones (TSH, Free T3, Free T4)
  • Sex hormones (testosterone, estrogen)
  • Cortisol & DHEA

Inflammation & Immune Markers

  • High-sensitivity CRP
  • Homocysteine
  • Inflammatory cytokines

Additional Testing

  • Vitamin D & B12 levels
  • Micronutrient analysis
  • Optional genetic screening
