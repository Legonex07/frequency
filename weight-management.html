Weight Management & Metabolic Optimization

Our scientifically validated weight-loss and metabolic optimization program uses evidence-based pharmacological agents, appetite modulation strategies, and comprehensive metabolic repair protocols. We combine the latest generation GLP-1/GIP agonists with personalized nutrition coaching and lifestyle modification to achieve sustainable, healthy weight loss.

This program is designed for individuals struggling with obesity, insulin resistance, or metabolic dysfunction. Every protocol is medically supervised and tailored to your specific metabolic profile and health goals.

Our Treatment Options

Latest generation GLP-1/GIP agonists for effective weight loss

Semaglutide

First-generation GLP-1 agonist with proven efficacy for sustainable weight loss. Reduces appetite and helps achieve 15-20% body weight reduction.

  • Once-weekly injection
  • 15-20% weight loss typically
  • Improved glucose control
  • Cardiovascular benefits

Tirzepatide

Dual GLP-1/GIP agonist with superior efficacy compared to single-agent therapies. Delivers 20-22% body weight reduction in clinical trials.

  • Once-weekly injection
  • 20-22% weight loss typically
  • Dual mechanism of action
  • Enhanced metabolic benefits

Retatrutide

First triple GLP-1/GIP/GCG agonist offering the most advanced pharmacological approach. Shows exceptional results in clinical development.

  • Weekly or bi-weekly dosing
  • Up to 24% weight loss
  • Triple mechanism action
  • Most advanced option available

Expected Results & Timeline

What to expect from your weight management program

Week 1-4

Initial Response

Appetite suppression begins, reduced food intake, initial weight loss of 2-4 kg. Some GI adjustment may occur (typically mild and temporary).

Week 4-12

Dose Optimization

Dose escalation to therapeutic level. Weight loss accelerates to 1-2 kg per week. Improved satiety and reduced cravings.

3-6 Months

Significant Progress

Sustained weight loss of 8-15% body weight. Improved metabolic markers, blood pressure, and glucose control. Increased energy levels.

6-12 Months

Major Transformation

15-20% body weight loss. Dramatic improvements in metabolic markers, joint health, and quality of life. Reversal of metabolic dysfunction.
