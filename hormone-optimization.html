Hormone Optimization Programs

Hormone balance is fundamental to optimal health, vitality, and quality of life. Our comprehensive hormone optimization programs use cutting-edge endocrinology protocols to diagnose and treat hormonal imbalances in both men and women.

Using advanced diagnostic testing and personalized treatment protocols, we restore hormonal balance to reduce fatigue, improve mood, enhance sexual function, and promote overall wellness. All programs are medically supervised with regular monitoring to ensure safety and efficacy.

Included in This Program

A comprehensive approach to hormonal health and optimization

Advanced Hormone Testing

Comprehensive endocrinology-grade blood work including testosterone, estrogen, progesterone, thyroid, cortisol, DHEA, and other key hormonal markers.

Male Testosterone Programs

TRT (Testosterone Replacement Therapy), Enclomiphene for natural testosterone stimulation, and HCG therapy to maintain fertility and testicular function.

Female Hormone Therapy

Estrogen, progesterone, and adrenal support therapy. Bioidentical hormones for menopause, perimenopause, and other hormonal conditions.

Thyroid Optimization

Complete thyroid assessment and treatment. TSH, free T3, free T4 optimization to improve metabolism, energy, and overall health.

Growth Hormone Treatment

Growth hormone deficiency assessment and therapy (if clinically indicated) to improve body composition, strength, and longevity.

Quarterly Monitoring & Adjustment

Regular follow-up testing and dose adjustments every 3 months to ensure optimal hormone levels, safety, and therapeutic outcomes.

Ideal For

Low Testosterone/Andropause

Men experiencing fatigue, low libido, mood changes, reduced muscle mass, and other symptoms of testosterone deficiency.

Menopause & Perimenopause

Women experiencing hot flashes, mood swings, sleep disturbance, and other menopausal symptoms requiring hormone support.

Thyroid Disorders

Individuals with hypothyroidism, hyperthyroidism, or thyroid dysfunction requiring optimization and medical management.

Fatigue & Low Vitality

Chronic fatigue, low energy, or reduced performance that may be caused by hormonal imbalance requiring comprehensive evaluation.
